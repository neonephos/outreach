# Template Work Packages for Social Media Use

The following items are predefined work package templates that are used throughout the Social Media documents. Using reusable components for standard bodies of work eases simplifies development of the Social Media Strategy.

>[!NOTE]
> 

## List of Work Packages

> [!NOTE]
> Each work package has an associated cost. While for some packages *man hours* might seem a more sensible measure of cost, every effort listed here is calculated in Euro. This eases cost comparisons between work packages.

### LinkedIn Campaign

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
|Responsible| @neonephos/outreach-social-media|
|Platform|[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/neonephos-foundation)|
|Cost | €100/week |
| Requirements  | Content to post about |
|Outputs| LinkedIn Posts|

The LinkedIn campaign accompanies posts from the NeoNephos account on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/neonephos-foundation).

This package includes:

- Ideation and creation of new posts.
- Reposting, commenting and liking of related content.
- Creating banners and associated posting media.

Posts frequency is set as appropriate in the eyes of the Social Media SIG.

### Video Series

| Item | Details |
|---|---|
| Responsible | @neonephos/outreach-social-media|
| Platform |[YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@neonephosfoundation)|
| Cost | €10000 |
| Requirements  | Content to post about |
| Outputs | Videos |

A video series about a predefined topic is created. Videos are uploaded to the [NeoNephos YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@neonephosfoundation).

This package includes:

- Ideation and development of the videos
- Potential travel
- Uploading the results on YouTube
- Promoting the results on additional platforms such as LinkedIn

> [!NOTE]
> Video series take a lot of effort to produce and should therefore be used sparringly.
