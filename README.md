# Welcome to the Outreach Repository

👋 Welcome! If you are looking for the official repository of the NeoNephos Outreach Committee you have come to the right place!

## What do we do?

According to [4a.ii of the NeoNephos Charter](https://cdn.platform.linuxfoundation.org/agreements/neonephos-foundation.pdf?__hstc=81619592.11739e1f4ca03192f4e918409f725053.1748961872693.1755169332458.1755604645613.29&__hssc=81619592.2.1755604645613&__hsfp=1879682750), the Outreach Committee is defined as such:

> The Outreach Committee will be responsible for the design, development, and 
execution of community outreach efforts on behalf of the Governing Board. The 
Outreach Committee is expected to coordinate closely with the Governing Board and 
technical communities to maximize the outreach and visibility of the Technical 
Project throughout the ecosystem.

Explicit work items derived from this abstract definition are:

- Proposing events to the Governing Board
- Managing the NeoNephos Ambassador Program
- Managing Merchandise
- Designing and implementing a marketing and PR strategy
- Managing Social Media accounts

## Meetings

Monthly on Wednesdays @3PM CET/CEST.

All meetings are managed by the Linux Foundation Project Control Center.

> The members of the Outreach Committee are representatives of Premium and General Members.

> [!TIP]
> Participation in our SIGs is decided by the SIGs themselves. [Check the SIGs](https://github.com/neonephos/outreach/tree/main/topics) to see their membership requirements.

## How to get involved

Our committee and working group meetings are open to the people defined in [4a.i of our charter](https://cdn.platform.linuxfoundation.org/agreements/neonephos-foundation.pdf?__hstc=81619592.11739e1f4ca03192f4e918409f725053.1748961872693.1755169332458.1755604645613.29&__hssc=81619592.2.1755604645613&__hsfp=1879682750) and a handful of invitees - yet! In the meantime, feel free to ask questions here, on [Zulip](https://linuxfoundation.zulipchat.com/#narrow/channel/525732-neonephos-discussion/topic/channel.20events/with/558483910) or ping our members directly.
