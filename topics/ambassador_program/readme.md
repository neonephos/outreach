# Welcome to the Ambassador Program

👋 Ahoj! If you are looking for the official Ambassador Program repository you have come to the right place!

> [!TIP]
> Formal Membership in the NeoNephos Outreach Committee is not required to be involved in this working group. 

## What do we do?

As defined in our [Ambassador Program Overview Document](documentation/ambassador_role.md):

> NeoNephos Ambassadors promote our foundation and its projects through participation in events, hosting workshops, creating tutorials or contributing other content. They are a point of contact and knowledge for people interested in NeoNephos and play a fundamental part in our community outreach strategy.

## How to become an Ambassador?

Read up on the [Ambassador Program Overview Document](documentation/ambassador_role.md).

## What benefits do I get as an Ambassador?

This is currently being fleshed out. But you can expect the usual goodies, such as:

- Access to free Merchandise
- Financial support for promotional activities such as attending events 
- Credly Badge

## Point of Contact

The best way to get in touch is to [raise an issue in this repository](https://github.com/neonephos/outreach/issues/new/choose).

Our current chairperson is [Annika Opitz](https://github.com/kub3anni) who also has an observer seat at the [NeoNephos Governing Board](https://neonephos.org/governing_board).
All our current members and their voting statuses are kept up-to-date [here](https://neonephos.org/outreach_committee).

## How to get involved in this Special Interest Group

Formal Membership in the NeoNephos Outreach Committee is not required for this working group. 


> [!TIP]
> Participation in our SIGs is decided by the SIGs themselves. [Check the SIGs](https://github.com/neonephos/outreach/tree/main/topics) to see their membership requirements.
